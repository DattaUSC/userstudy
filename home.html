About Us

We're regular people looking for regular solutions to some big problems in how we use our money.

Our goal is to interview 100 people.

Each interview can take anywhere from 20 - 45 minutes.
It's all about how much you want to share with us about the most
positive and negative experiences you've had with how your money is managed.

Example Interview Questions...

You can say as much or as little as you feel like! We don't ask anything personal about your finances. We're more interested in how you manage payments.

“What do you wish was different about online banking?”

“Do you have student loans? What tools, if any, are you using to pay them off?”

“What's a great customer service experience? What's made it great?”
